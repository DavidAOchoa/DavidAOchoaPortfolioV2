Carton

Web app that keeps track of all food in your kitchen and displays analytics to help you manage your budget and waste less food. Carton is a smart grocery list that will save you money, save you time, and save you food poisoning by keeping track of the food in your kitchen and various analytics such as how much you use of a certain item, how often you have to buy it, how much you spend, and how long a certain item is good for.
