Gemini

Gemini is a mobile app that uses website scanning technology to help users discover designer-style items at an affordable price. The app finds look-alikes of expensive designer items and offers different tiers for various price ranges, allowing users to choose how much they want to spend. For example, if a designer shoe costs $400, Gemini can help find a look alike for as low as $40 or $100. With Gemini, users can enjoy the styles they love without breaking the bank.
